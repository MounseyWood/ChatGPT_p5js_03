3D Cloth Simulation

This demo has two simulation modes:
• Plane: A pinned cloth swatch.
• Draped: A cloth placed horizontally over a sphere, collides with sphere & floor.

Simulation Mode:
Plane = pinned cloth in free space.
Draped = cloth starts horizontal above sphere & floor.
Interaction Mode:
"Rotate" to move the camera; "Drag" to pick cloth points. (Use "Reset Orientation" first for best dragging.)
Pinning Mode:
Only relevant if in "Plane" mode.
Damping:
Controls energy loss (Off = 1.0).
Iterations:
Constraint relaxations per frame (Off = 1).
Stretch:
Affects cloth extension (Off = 1.0).
Shear:
Resists diagonal distortion (Off = 1.0).
Bending:
Resistance to sharp folds (Off = 1.0).
Weight:
Downward pull (Off = 1.0).
Wind X:
Wind force along X (Off = 0).
Wind Y:
Wind force along Y (Off = 0).
Wind Z:
Wind force along Z (Off = 0).
Wind Buffer:
Scales overall wind amplitude (Off = 1.0).
Gravity:
Downward acceleration (Off = 0).
Shading Mode:
Gray-scale fill based on vertical position.
