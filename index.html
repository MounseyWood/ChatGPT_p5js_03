3D Cloth Simulation

Click on the cloth (in the canvas) to drag points in 3D (drag works on the fixed Z‐plane). Use the radio buttons below to toggle parameters On/Off.

Pinning Mode:
Choose whether the cloth is pinned along the top edge or only at its corners.
Damping:
Controls energy loss. (Off = 1.0)
Iterations:
Number of constraint relaxations per frame. (Off = 1)
Stretch:
Affects how much the cloth can extend. (Off = 1.0)
Shear:
Resists diagonal distortion. (Off = 1.0)
Bending:
Controls resistance to sharp folds. (Off = 1.0)
Weight:
Modifies the downward pull. (Off = 1.0)
Wind X:
Wind force along the X-axis. (Off = 0)
Wind Y:
Wind force along the Y-axis. (Off = 0)
Wind Z:
Wind force along the Z-axis. (Off = 0)
Wind Buffer:
Scales overall wind oscillation amplitude. (Off = 1.0)
Gravity:
Sets the downward acceleration. (Off = 0)
Shading Mode:
Fills each cell with a gray-scale color based on vertical position.
